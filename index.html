Alexander the Great

Timeline of Alexander the Great

  • 356 BC - Born at Pella, Macedonia, to King Philip II and Olympias
  • 336 BC - Becomes ruler of Macedonia
  • 334 BC - Wins Battle of the Granicus River against Darius III of Persia
  • 333 BC - Wins Battle at Issus against Darius
  • 331 BC - Founds Alexandria. Wins Battle of Gaugamela against Darius
  • 329 BC - Crosses Hindu Kush; goes to Bactria and crosses the Oxus river and then to Samarkand.
  • 327 BC - Marries Roxane; begins march to India
  • 326 BC - Wins Battle of river Hydaspes against Porus; Bucephalus dies
  • 323 BC - Dies at Babylon in the palace of Nebuchadnezzar II
